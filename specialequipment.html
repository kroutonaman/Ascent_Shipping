Special Equipments

Over dimension cargo does not always need Breakbulk or conventional loading, specialised containers are used to safely lash the cargo for general liner loading, ASCENT has own special Equipments also represent other liners who owns special Equipments and offer service to various destinations. Our skilled operation team helps you to minimise the number of containers used for combination of cargo, working with cargo size, weight, Container stability, Pay load capacity and Terminal capacity every thing in mind.
